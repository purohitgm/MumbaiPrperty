About

We monitor thousands of luxury real estate listings across Mumbai to detect price reductions in real time. When sellers lower prices on sea-facing apartments, penthouses, or premium villas, those listings appear here first. Price drops are tracked across Bandra, Juhu, Worli, Lower Parel, Malabar Hill, Prabhadevi, and BKC.

How it works
1We scan luxury Mumbai listings (₹5Cr+) from MagicBricks, 99acres, Housing.com and NoBroker every hour
2Prices are compared against our history database — drops ≥ 3% are flagged instantly
3Click any row to open the original listing on the source platform
4Backend refreshes automatically every 60 minutes. Hit the ↻ button to force a refresh.
